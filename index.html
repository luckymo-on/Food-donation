Food Donation &

WeShare is a non-profit organization
that facilitates the donation of excess food from individuals, businesses, and events
to local charities and food banks, reducing food waste and providing for those in need.
Through their efforts, WeShare is making a significant impact
on the lives of individuals and families struggling with food insecurity.
